Frequently asked questions

How do I play?
Tap to drop a Plopple into the jar. When two of the same kind touch, they merge into the next, bigger Plopple. Keep merging to climb all the way up the ladder — just don't let the jar overflow.
What are the ads for, and are they optional?
Ads are always optional. Watching a short video can claim a free coin gift, continue a run, or double your score — and every one starts with a clear choice that includes a "No thanks" button. Ads are non-personalized.
The flashing during a near-miss bothers me. Can I turn it down?
Yes. Open Settings → Reduce Flashing. It replaces the danger flash with a steady highlight and turns off screen shake. It switches on automatically if your device has "Reduce Motion" enabled.
Does my progress save? How do I reset it?
Your coins, collection, and best score save automatically on your device. To start over, delete and reinstall the app (progress is stored only on your device, not in the cloud).
Is the game safe for kids? What data is collected?
PloppleDrop is built for a young audience: it collects no personal information, shows only non-personalized, general-audience ads, and never asks to track you. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.
