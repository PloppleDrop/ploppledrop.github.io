PloppleDrop — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 23 June 2026

Who we are

PloppleDrop (“the app”, “we”, “us”) is a casual mobile merge game. This policy explains what information the app handles and how. Contact: ploppledrop@gmail.com.

Our approach

PloppleDrop is designed for a young audience, so we deliberately collect as little as possible:

  • No account, no login. You do not create an account or give us your name, email, or phone number to play.
  • No personal data collection by us. Your game progress (high score, coins, unlocked creatures, cosmetics, settings) is stored only on your device. We do not upload your progress, and we cannot see it.
  • Anonymous, aggregate stats only. We keep global tallies of in-game actions (for example, total Plopples dropped or merged across all players) to see how the game is played. These are aggregate totals only — they contain no personal information and no device identifiers, are not linked to you, and are not used to track you. We do not embed any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

On-device storage

The app saves your progress locally using your device’s standard app storage. This data never leaves your device and is removed if you delete the app.

Anonymous gameplay statistics

To understand how PloppleDrop is played, the app keeps simple global counters of in-game actions — for example, how many Plopples are dropped, how many merges happen, or how often an ad fails to load — and periodically sends them as aggregate totals to a small counter service we run.

These counters are anonymous and aggregate by construction: they are plain running totals (e.g. “drops today: 12,034”). They include no account, no name, no device identifier, no IP address, and no per-person or per-session records — just numbers added to a shared tally. Because nothing is linked to you or your device, this is not “tracking,” needs no tracking permission, and does not change our non-personalized advertising. You cannot be identified from this data.

Advertising

PloppleDrop shows ads through Google AdMob to support the free game. To respect our young audience:

  • The app requests child-directed treatment from AdMob, limiting ads to family-appropriate, non-personalized content where required.
  • Ads are never shown during active gameplay — only on the main menu, the game-over screen, and optional “watch to earn” reward moments you choose to start.
  • Ads are always non-personalized and capped at a G (General audiences) content rating. We never request cross-app tracking, so the app never shows the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt — there is no tracking to allow or deny.

Google’s handling of advertising data is governed by Google’s own policy: policies.google.com/privacy and AdMob & children.

Children’s privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Consistent with COPPA and the EU’s protections for minors:

  • We collect no personal identifiers.
  • Advertising is configured for child-directed treatment.
  • There are no social features, chat, user-generated content, or external links to general-audience web content inside gameplay.

If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact ploppledrop@gmail.com and we will address it promptly.

Purchases

The in-game shop uses only cosmetic items bought with in-game coins earned by playing or by watching optional reward ads. Coins and cosmetics are non-personal and stored on-device.

Your choices

  • Reset your data by deleting and reinstalling the app.
  • Ad personalization is off by design — ads are always non-personalized, so there is no ad-tracking setting you need to change.

Changes

We may update this policy; the “Effective date” above will change accordingly. Material changes will be reflected in an app update.
